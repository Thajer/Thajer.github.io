Ever thought of the fun games you can play with your dog? Here are some fun games that you can play with your dog

Fetch

Fetch is a really fun game in which your dog can get some exercise and have fun. You play fetch by throwing a stick or ball somewhere far and your dog runs behined it and brings it back to you. If you have a really small puppy, then you can roll the ball on the floor. You can also use tennis balls, rubber bone toys or rubber sticks to play fetch with your dog.

Treasure Hunt

Treasure hunt is a really good and fun game. As dogs have really strong sence of smell, they remember every scent very distinctly. This is what you should do, take a few things, make your dog smell it and hide them in different corners of your house. Make sure that your dog doesn't get too confused looking for them. After that set your dog free and lit him/her find what they're looking for. You can play treasure hunt both in your house or even outside your house. This can even help your dog on running.

Frisbee

Frisbee is a very common game and is kind of like fetch but it's more fun than fetch. This game is
good for lazy dogs makes them run and jump alot. This is a very 'full-of-energy' game and a good one
for dogs. So what are you waiting for? Go get your frisbee and start playing!

Hide and Seek

Hide and seek is a game that children and dogs enjoy. The rules are quite simple and make sure
that you don't hide somewhere where it is impossible for your dog to find you. Make it a little easier for them.
