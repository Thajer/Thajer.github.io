There are multiple companies which make dog food. But before going to the companies that make dog food, remember that your food is not always right for the dog. Here are some people foods that are safe for dogs:

    People foods that are safe for dogs:

  • Carrots
  • Apples
  • White Rice
  • Dairy Products
  • Fish, Chicken, Pork, Turkey
  • Green Beans
  • Cucumbers
  • Bananas
  • Watermelon (make sure to remove seeds)

    People foods that are not safe for dogs:

  • Chocolates, coffee, caffeine
  • Avocado
  • Cherries
  • Raisins and grapes
  • Citrus fruits
  • Cinnamon
  • Nuts
  • Ice cream
  • Onions, garlic, chives
